# Documentation Structure

This folder contains all research, design, copy, and strategy documentation for bancualex.com.

## Folder Organization

### `/research`
Market research, keyword analysis, and niche validation studies.

- **keyword-research.md** — Ahrefs keyword data organized by tiers (direct niche match, high volume, question-based, niche authority)
- **keyword-clusters.md** — Keywords grouped into 5 strategic clusters (Burnout, Feeling Stuck, Clarity & Direction, Mental Loops & Overthinking, Coaching Education)
- **niche-validation-analysis.md** — Deep analysis using Painkiller vs Vitamin framework, Hair on Fire test, willingness to pay research. Ranks clusters by problem severity, money, and urgency.
- **successful-digital-products-research.md** — Market analysis of successful digital products with revenue data, pricing strategies, and product recommendations.
- **landing-page-research.md** — Research on effective landing page patterns and copy strategies.

### `/design`
Brand identity, visual design, and style guidelines.

- **brand-identity-colors.md** — Color palette definitions and usage guidelines (Slate & Green system)
- **font-pairings.md** — Typography system (Lora for display/headings, Inter for body)

### `/copy`
Copywriting iterations for key pages.

- **coaching-copy-v1.md** — First version of coaching page copy
- **coaching-copy-v2.md** — Revised version of coaching page copy

### `/seo`
SEO strategy, guides, and best practices.

- **blog-post-seo-guide.md** — Checklist and guidelines for SEO-optimized blog posts
- **blog-publishing-strategy.md** — Content publishing roadmap and strategy

## Quick Reference

**Planning a new blog post?**
1. Check `/research/keyword-clusters.md` for target keywords
2. Follow `/seo/blog-post-seo-guide.md` checklist
3. Reference `/seo/blog-publishing-strategy.md` for timing

**Validating a product idea?**
1. Review `/research/niche-validation-analysis.md` for frameworks
2. Check `/research/successful-digital-products-research.md` for pricing/models
3. Use Painkiller vs Vitamin test (hair on fire, cost of inaction)

**Updating brand/design?**
1. Reference `/design/brand-identity-colors.md` for colors
2. Reference `/design/font-pairings.md` for typography
3. Maintain consistency with existing system

## Key Insights Summary

**Top Priority Niches (from validation analysis):**
1. **Burnout** (9.5/10) — Strongest painkiller, highest WTP
2. **Indecisiveness/Overthinking** (9.0/10) — Huge keyword volume, perfect for digital products
3. **Feeling Stuck** (8.5/10) — Best for 1-on-1 coaching, premium market

**SEO Strategy:**
- Target Easy KD keywords first
- Top 20 keywords identified in keyword-clusters.md
- Every article links to coaching page with booking CTA

**Digital Product Pricing (from market research):**
- Low-ticket: $17-$97 (templates, toolkits)
- Mid-ticket: $297-$997 (courses, programs)
- High-ticket: $997-$2,000 (group cohorts)
- Premium: $3K-$15K (1-on-1 coaching)
